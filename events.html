EVENTS

ICEBREAKING AND HANDHOLDING SESSION

A kickstart event, the agenda of which was to make the girls understand the purpose of their being in SHE. This is where the girls got to know each other and their abilities and skills.

LEAD THE LEADERS

Lead the Leaders was a 2 -day residential camp organized by us under the HR Cell of GCEK for the benefit of 30 selected girl students of the SHE group. The program consisted of talk sessions by many professionals from different career sectors of which some were centered around the importance of inner well-being, physical health as well as maintaining good emotional intelligence while few were related to career opportunities and life as a research scientist and entrepreneur.

SHE WEB DEVELOPMENT BOOTCAMP

"Mission: Build Website” was an online Website development Bootcamp held for the female student community. This online camp opted a method of traditional learning along with daily practical tasks. The basics of HTML and CSS followed by a brief introduction to Java were the main focus. 40+ students participated out of which 20+ students took part in the Interactive Website building Contest that followed. The Bootcamp was successful in establishing a forum wherein the girls could actively pose their doubts with their peers and have an active exchange of ideas.

TAKE DE BAIT

This was an Inter-Collegiate Debate competition conducted for the female engineering students of the state under the topic “Is the pandemic work culture detrimental to women in tech”. The debate was judged by Aisha Nazia, who is a Women in Tech- Mechanical Engineering Graduate, founder of Start Change. Today, Growth Hacker and a blogger. The event saw the participation of over 30+ active girl students across the state.

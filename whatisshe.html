What Is SHE?

The SHE program is steadily empowering girl students across the state, helping them recognize their potential and the support they need to become leaders in technical fields. The coordinators have effectively leveraged the expertise of our resourceful alumni, ensuring their active role in shaping the program. Key contributors include the dedicated teaching fraternity of GCEK, resource persons from leading agencies, and experts from various domains. With strong backing from the PTA and the HRD Cell of GCEK, SHE continues to drive meaningful change, fostering a statewide network of support and excellence.

We launched SHE in early 2019 with just a few participants, and today, it has expanded statewide, with thousands of girl students actively engaged in elevating SHE to new heights, bringing it on par with other leading student organizations across institutions.

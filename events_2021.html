EVENTS

CANCER AWARENESS

The event is conducted by SHE GCEK collaborating with Malabar Cancer Care Society, as a part of WHO anniversary event of mission ‘Elimination of cervical cancer 2030’ . It is an awareness session on cervical cancer handled by Dr Harsha G.
The event was held on google meet. Around 80 people joined for the session.

CYBER SECURITY AWARENESS

SHE GCEK coordinated the Cybersecurity Awareness Session in collaboration with WISE (Women in Security Excelling) IBM. The session gave the students the opportunity to interact with IBM tech experts, it also included fun and engaging group activities and discussions live and demo sessions by experts

INISHEATE

SHE GCEK organized an orientation program, iniSHEate to the vibrant girls of the 2021 batch. IniSHEate commenced with a formal introduction from our principal. Dr. Vandana Sreedharan, SHE coordinator delivered a very insightful talk on SHE and also welcomed all the freshers to the SHE community. We conducted fun games for the girls to break the ice and also our alumni shared their wonderful experience with SHE to the whole crowd.

WORKSHOP ON CANVA

SHE GCEK organized an interactive TWO DAY WORKSHOP ON CANVA. The session covered all the basics of canva and involved live tutorials and tasks which will enhance designing skills.

Falicitation Ceremony

SHE GCEK conducted a felicitation ceremony for Ms.Vaidehi C K who bagged the best student coordinator award, ISTE Kerala Section. The event was held on Google Meet.

A Get-Together

As part of International Women's Day SHE GCEK conducted a get together of SHE, we connect members and the lady faculties of GCEK.
The chief guest of the event was Dr. Indu K Murthy, Senior Head - Climate, Environment and sustainability, CSTEP.
On this occasion we also proudly communicated the honourable achievement by Dr Sudha U PV, batch of 1994 GCEK, who is being selected for the Vanitha Ratnam Awards for 2021. We also delightfully announced the winners the finalists of WE4U first round.

LEAP

WE4U Award Distribution Ceremony was organized by SHE GCEK. The function was inaugurated by P.K. Sreemathi Teacher, Former MP. It was followed by inaguration of LEAP by Dr. Vandana Sreedharan, Coordinator,SHE GCEK for the final year students.

ALOHA

SHE GCEK came together to bid the farewell party 'Aloha' to the outgoing seniors from 2018 batch. We had a wholesome run through the memory lane and we thank all you seniors for helping us grow.

INVESTITURE CEREMONY

SHE GCEK conducted an INVESTITURE CEREMONY , to welcome the vibrant members of our new EXECOM 2022 -2023.

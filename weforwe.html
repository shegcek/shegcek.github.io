ABOUT
WE FOR WE (Women Engineers for Women Entrepreneurs) is a virtual Makethon aimed at bringing together budding women engineers to present solutions to some of the challenges that women, especially newbie entrepreneurs face organised by SHE GCEK in association with Kerala Startup Mission.

About
WE FOR WE (Women Engineers for Women Entrepreneurs) is a virtual Makeathon organized by SHE GCEK in association with Kerala Startup Mission aimed at bringing together budding women engineers to present solutions to some of the challenges that women, especially newbie entrepreneurs face.
Themes
  • SHE SUPPORTS FOR WOMEN SMALL BUSINESS OWNERS
    Small business owners who are restricted to use just social media for selling their services/goods face many challenges in terms of delivery of products,promotion, ads and visibility. Solutions should focus on addressing these issues- either individually or wholly.

  • WOMEN SAFETY SOLUTIONS
    Even though there are existing solutions to ensure the safety of women, many compromise on ease of use, reliability and fast response to immediate threats. Solutions should focus on being unrestrictive, safe to use and ensure privacy to users.

  • SKILL UP FOR WOMEN FREELANCERS - SHELANCERS
    After taking a career break, women find themselves not having the necessary skills and tools required to re-enter the workforce. Solutions should focus on addressing this issue for women re-entering the workforce as well as those who wish to start from scratch to learn a skill.

FAQ
  • What is WE FOR WE?

    • An inter-collegiate Makeathon organized by SHE-Gcek in association with Kerala Startup Mission.
    • Exclusive to female engineering students.

  • What is the expected outcome?

    • Converting your idea into full-fledged product/service that is market-ready.

  • What is the Theme?

    • We aim to bring together budding women engineers to present solutions to some of the challenges that women, especially newbies entrepreneurs face.

  • What are the benefits of joining the program?
    • Startup Incubation
      Selected teams will be considered for startup incubation from Kerala Startup Mission.
    • Mentorship
      Guidance and mentoring will be provided to the selected teams inorder to estabilish their product.
